## Filing note
Do not overstate legal conclusions.
Use phrases like:
- "official-project impersonation appears likely"
- "investment inducement structure is visible"
- "brand confusion with Kbank's publicly reported K-STABLE naming is possible"
Avoid phrases like:
- "criminal liability is proven"
- "trademark infringement is conclusively established"
